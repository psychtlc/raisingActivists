      Who I am

      A 'nasty' woman

      I am a mom to 5 children and I make sure to bring passionate discourse to our breakfast and dinner table each and every day. #blacklivesmatter #intersectionalfeminist #sexpositive #checkyourprivilege

      A lover of learning, I spend my 'free' time learning how our family can live a more sustainable responsible life. I bake and naturally levin all of our own bread, raise chickens, compost, and have begun an attempt at gardening. So far the only things successfully grown have been various herbs and a few tomatoes.       crackerjack project manager

      Organizing, communicating and facilitating the schedules and activities of 5 children in 4 separate schools, 2 non-custodial parents, and a mini-farm/homestead is an exhausting but rewarding endeavor. It's no easy feat, but Google Apps, Trello, and Wunderlist make it manageable.  I'm also a certified Scrum Master with real Project Management experience, but that is not quite as interesting!
